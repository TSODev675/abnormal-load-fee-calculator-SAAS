Permit Application Process

Follow these steps to apply for a permit:

  1. Fill out the permit application form with the required details.
  2. Attach necessary documents such as route maps and load specifications.
  3. Submit the application and wait for approval.
  4. Once approved, download your permit and keep it with you during transportation.
